Phrases

BIS = Best items for a unit “BIS for Irelia is red buff”

Caps = Refers to how good the comp is late game “This board caps high”

Neeko = A champion duplicator “I can neeko my Morgana to 2 star her”

Slow roll = Roll excess gold above 50 for a 3 star unit “Slowroll for 3 star Bard”

WIS = Worst items for a unit “Guinsoo Kaisa is WIS”
